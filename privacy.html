Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 13, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 13, 2026

1. Overview

Momentum Catalyst Advisory ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Bookkeeping Audit Application (the "Application"), an internal tool used to access and analyze QuickBooks Online financial data for authorized business purposes. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with the Application.

By using the Application, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Access

The Application connects to QuickBooks Online via Intuit's OAuth 2.0 authorization framework. With your explicit authorization, the Application may access the following categories of QuickBooks Online data:

  • Chart of accounts and account balances
  • Financial reports (Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet, Aging reports)
  • Transaction records and lists
  • Customer and vendor records
  • Company information

We do not access payment card data, banking credentials, or any information beyond the scope of the QuickBooks Accounting permission granted during OAuth authorization.

3. How We Use the Information

Data accessed through the Application is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Conducting internal financial audits and reviews
  • Identifying bookkeeping issues and optimization opportunities
  • Generating advisory recommendations for the authorized account holder

We do not use your QuickBooks data for marketing, advertising, or any purpose beyond the scope of your direct advisory engagement with Momentum Catalyst Advisory.

4. Data Retention and Storage

The Application is designed for session-based analysis. We do not permanently store raw QuickBooks data on external servers. Any data retrieved during a session is used within that session and is not written to persistent third-party storage.

Any reports or summaries generated from your data are retained only as necessary to provide the advisory services you have requested and are handled in accordance with our client confidentiality obligations.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your QuickBooks data with any third parties except:

  • Intuit Inc. — as the platform through which authorization and data access occurs, subject to Intuit's Privacy Statement
  • As required by law — if disclosure is required by applicable law, regulation, or legal process

6. Security

Access to your QuickBooks Online account is governed by Intuit's OAuth 2.0 protocol. OAuth tokens are used only for the duration of the authorized session and are not shared with unauthorized parties. You may revoke the Application's access to your QuickBooks Online account at any time via your Intuit account settings at accounts.intuit.com.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke the Application's access to your QuickBooks data at any time
  • Request information about what data was accessed during a session
  • Request deletion of any summaries or reports we hold on your behalf

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Children's Privacy

The Application is intended for use by business professionals and is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the Application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
